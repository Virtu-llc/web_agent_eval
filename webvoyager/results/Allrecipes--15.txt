Ingredients (original yield: 12 servings)
- 2 individual packages graham crackers
- 3 cups cold milk
- 2 (3 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 (16 ounce) package prepared chocolate frosting

Step-by-step instructions
1. Gather all ingredients.
2. Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch pan with a single layer of graham crackers.
3. Combine cold milk and pudding mix in a large bowl; whisk briskly for 2 minutes.
4. Mix the whipped topping into the pudding mixture until combined.
5. Spread half of the pudding mixture over the graham cracker layer.
6. Top with another layer of graham crackers. Spread the remaining pudding over that layer. Top with a final layer of graham crackers.
7. Frost the top layer evenly with the chocolate frosting.
8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the graham crackers to soften.
