Recipe: Spanish Paella
URL: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/12728/paella-i/
Rating and reviews on page: 4.7 (177) with 134 Reviews
Total Time: 1 hr 30 mins (Prep 45 mins + Cook 45 mins)
Servings: 6

Includes shrimp and mussels
- Yes. Ingredients list includes: 12 large shrimp and 1 pound mussels.

Ingredients (original 6 servings)
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 4 ounces Spanish chorizo, casing discarded and cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 (12 ounce) package uncooked Arborio rice
- 5 cups chicken broth (added gradually; see directions)
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 pinch saffron
- Salt, to taste
- Ground black pepper, to taste
- 2 squid, cleaned and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 1/2 cup frozen green peas
- 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
- 1/4 cup chopped Italian flat-leaf parsley
- 8 slices lemon, for garnish

Overview of preparation steps
1) Base aromatics and meats: Heat olive oil in a paella pan over medium heat. Sauté onion, garlic, and red pepper for a few minutes. Add chorizo, chicken, and rice; cook 2–3 minutes.
2) Build the rice: Stir in 3 1/2 cups chicken stock, the white wine, thyme leaves, and saffron; season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer ~15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste the rice and continue adding stock 1/2 cup at a time (up to a total of 5 cups) until rice is cooked.
3) Add seafood and veg: Stir in squid, tomatoes, and peas; cook ~2 minutes.
4) Finish and rest: Arrange shrimp and mussels on top, cover with foil, and rest 3–5 minutes until the shrimp are cooked and mussels open.
5) Garnish and serve: Scatter parsley over the top and serve in the paella pan with lemon wedges.
