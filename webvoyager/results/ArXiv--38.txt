ArXiv Blog latest post
- Title: Attention Authors: Upcoming Scheduled Maintenance to arxiv.org
- URL: https://blog.arxiv.org/2025/09/16/arxiv-org-maintenance-sep-17-2025/
- Author: Charles Frankston
- Publication date: September 16, 2025
- Categories: arXiv support, arXiv updates

Summary of the post
- arXiv will perform scheduled database maintenance on Wednesday, September 17, 2025, from 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM EDT.
- During this window, browsing, viewing, and searching papers on arxiv.org will remain available.
- All actions requiring login will be disabled, including submissions, account editing, and endorsements.
- Authors should not plan submissions during this period, as login and submission will be unavailable while servers are upgraded.
- The maintenance is expected to last two hours. For real-time status updates, users can check https://status.arxiv.org.
