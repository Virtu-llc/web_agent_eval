Times
- Prep: 15 mins
- Cook: 30 mins
- Additional: 10 mins
- Total: 55 mins

Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups granulated white sugar
- 3/4 cup good-quality unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup hot water or coffee
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Brief overview of preparation steps
- Whisk dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, salt) together in a large bowl.
- Add wet ingredients (hot water or coffee, oil, vanilla) and stir until well blended.
- Pour batter into a greased, parchment-lined 9x13-inch baking dish; spread evenly.
- Bake at 350°F (175°C) until the top is no longer shiny and the center feels just set, about 30 minutes.
- Let cool at least 10 minutes before cutting into 15 squares.
