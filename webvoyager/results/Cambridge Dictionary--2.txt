Word: ubiquitous

UK English
- Pronunciation: /juːˈbɪk.wɪ.təs/
- Definition: seeming to be everywhere:
- Example: Leather is very much in fashion this season, as is the ubiquitous denim.

US English
- Pronunciation: /juːˈbɪk.wə.t̬əs/
- Definition: found or existing everywhere:
- Example: The eel grass limpet used to be ubiquitous on the New England coast.
